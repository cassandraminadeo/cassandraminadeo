Prompt

Activism that can be taken to the internet allows for such a successful widespread for the cause at hand. It can use several media platforms to share the cause to mass populations around the world. This is so successful because the access to internet is so much higher as everyday passes and allows for so many people to join the cause. The #Metoo movement has taken the world by storm is the best way possible to empower so many women to feel the confidence and safety to come forward about sexual assault. This movement made great prominence in 2019 that gave so many people the opportunity to come forward about the experiences that they went through.

The movement is credited with giving visibility to the scope of sexual violence within the United States and across the world. It is also defined by a push for accountability, including examining power structures in the workplace that had enabled misconduct, and, in some cases, renewed efforts to seek justice for survivors through criminal and civil court systems. In the first year of the movement, numerous prominent men lost their jobs after they were publicly accused of wrongdoing. (Brittain)
